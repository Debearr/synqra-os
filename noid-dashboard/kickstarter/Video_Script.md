## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Location
- Clean, minimal studio setup
- Matte black backdrop (or very dark gray)
- Soft key light (luxury aesthetic, not harsh)

### Wardrobe
- De Bear in all black (Rick Owens vibes)
- No logos, no distractions
- Minimal jewelry (if any)

### Camera
- 4K resolution minimum
- Shallow depth of field (luxury cinematic look)
- Stable tripod (no shaky cam)

### Audio
- Lavalier mic (clear voice)
- Light background music (minimal, luxury electronic)
- No distracting sound effects

### Editing Style
- Clean cuts, no flashy transitions
- Color grade: matte blacks, warm highlights, gold accents
- Subtitles (accessibility + silent autoplay)
- 16:9 aspect ratio (Kickstarter standard)
