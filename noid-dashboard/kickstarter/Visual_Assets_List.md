### Video

### Animation
- **After Effects** (for animated graphics, transitions)

## OUTSOURCING OPTIONS

If you don't have design/video skills:

### Design
- **Fiverr:** $50-150 per graphic (budget-friendly)
- **Upwork:** $30-80/hour (mid-tier designers)
- **Dribbble/Behance:** Hire top designers ($100-200/hour)

### Video
- **Fiverr:** $200-500 for 60-second video
- **Upwork:** $50-150/hour for video editing
- **Local videographer:** $500-1,500 for shoot + edit

### Total Budget Estimate
- **DIY:** $0 (time investment only)
- **Budget:** $500-1,000 (Fiverr for essentials)
- **Professional:** $2,500-5,000 (full production team)
