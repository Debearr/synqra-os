**We're being honest with you.**

### Technical Risks
- **Multi-AI orchestration is complex:** We've spent 18 months building this. It works, but scaling to 10,000+ users is non-trivial. We're confident, but there will be bugs.
- **AI model changes:** If OpenAI or Anthropic changes pricing or access, we'll need to adapt. We have fallback models, but it could impact cost structure.

### Market Risks
- **Competition:** Jasper, Copy.ai, and others could add luxury QA layers. Our moat is timing (first mover) + data (more customers = better training data).
- **Customer adoption:** Luxury brands are conservative. Convincing them to trust AI is a challenge. We're starting with creators to build proof points.

### Operational Risks
- **Scaling the team:** We're a small team (3 people today). Hiring world-class engineers for AI/ML is expensive and slow. We'll prioritize carefully.
- **Infrastructure costs:** Compute costs could spike if we underestimate usage. We have runway for 18 months, but rapid growth could pressure margins.

**Our Mitigation:**
- Conservative revenue projections (we're aiming for $8M ARR by end of 2026, but our model works at $3M)
- 18-month runway from this raise (gives us time to adjust)
- Strong technical team (founder is AI/ML engineer, not just a CEO)
