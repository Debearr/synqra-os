# Synqra Brand Color System

## PRIMARY COLORS

### Matte Black
- **Hex:** #0A0A0A
- **RGB:** 10, 10, 10
- **Usage:** Primary backgrounds, headers, bold statements

### Gold Accent
- **Hex:** #D4AF37
- **RGB:** 212, 175, 55
- **Usage:** Luxury highlights, CTAs, key metrics, emphasis

### Teal Signature
- **Hex:** #008B8B
- **RGB:** 0, 139, 139
- **Usage:** Brand signature, secondary accents, visual anchors

## NEUTRALS

### Charcoal
- **Hex:** #2B2B2B
- **RGB:** 43, 43, 43
- **Usage:** Secondary backgrounds, cards, containers

### Graphite
- **Hex:** #3C3C3C
- **RGB:** 60, 60, 60
- **Usage:** Borders, dividers, subtle elements

### Dust
- **Hex:** #F5F5F0
- **RGB:** 245, 245, 240
- **Usage:** Light backgrounds, contrast text, breathing room

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Headers
- **Primary:** Inter Bold
- **Secondary:** Helvetica Neue Bold
- **Usage:** Slide titles, section headers, bold statements

### Body Copy
- **Primary:** Inter Regular
- **Weights:** 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semi-bold)
- **Usage:** Main content, descriptions, data labels

### Editorial Accent
- **Font:** Times New Roman
- **Usage:** Pull quotes, founder story, luxury moments

## DESIGN STYLE PRINCIPLES

### Tesla Minimalism
- Clean lines, generous white space
- Let content breathe
- Remove unnecessary elements

### Tom Ford Precision
- Exact alignment and spacing
- Sophisticated restraint
- Every detail intentional

### Virgil Abloh Conceptual Restraint
- Bold ideas, minimal execution
- Cultural relevance without excess
- Function meets art

## COLOR PAIRING GUIDELINES

**High Contrast Luxury:**
- Matte Black (#0A0A0A) + Gold Accent (#D4AF37)
- Use for hero statements, pricing, key metrics

**Tech Sophistication:**
- Charcoal (#2B2B2B) + Teal Signature (#008B8B)
- Use for product features, technical diagrams

**Elegant Readability:**
- Dust (#F5F5F0) + Matte Black (#0A0A0A)
- Use for body content, data presentations

## APPLICATION NOTES

- Never use more than 3 colors per slide
- Gold is luxury - use sparingly for maximum impact
- Teal provides tech credibility without cold sterility
- Black grounds everything - anchor all designs to it
- Dust creates breathing room - don't fear white space
