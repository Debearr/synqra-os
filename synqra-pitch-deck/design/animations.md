# Synqra Pitch Deck Animation & Transition Specifications

## Design Philosophy

**Principle:** Luxury minimalism — restraint over excess, precision over flourish, architectural over decorative.

**Inspiration:**
- Tom Ford campaigns (deliberate, confident pacing)
- Tesla product reveals (minimal motion, maximum impact)
- Virgil Abloh exhibitions (conceptual, not literal)

**Animation Rules:**
1. Never animate for animation's sake
2. Every motion must serve narrative purpose
3. Slower is more confident (no rush)
4. Fade > slide > zoom (sophistication over flash)
5. Hold on key moments (let insights breathe)

---

## Global Transition Standards

### Between-Slide Transitions

**Standard Transition:** Cross-fade (0.6s duration)
- From: Current slide fades to 90% opacity
- To: Next slide fades in from 10% to 100%
- Overlap: 0.3s (smooth, not jarring)
- Easing: Ease-in-out (natural, not linear)

**When to Use:** 90% of slide transitions

---

**Dramatic Transition:** Full fade to black (1.2s total)
- From: Current slide fades to black (0.6s)
- Hold: Black screen (0.2s)
- To: Next slide fades from black (0.4s)

**When to Use:**
- Before major section shifts (Problem → Solution, Traction → Market)
- Before "The Ask" slide (build anticipation)
- Before Closing slide (full circle moment)

---

### On-Slide Element Animation

**Text Reveals:**
- Fade in from 0% to 100% opacity (0.4s)
- Stagger: 0.15s between elements
- No sliding motion (fade only)
- Never letter-by-letter (unless intentional luxury moment)

**Visual Elements:**
- Charts/graphs: Draw-in animation (1.2s) with 0.5s delay after slide loads
- Icons: Fade in (0.3s) after text
- Images: Fade in (0.6s), no zoom or pan
- Callout boxes: Fade + subtle scale (0.95 → 1.0) over 0.5s

**Data Animation:**
- Numbers: Count-up effect (1.5s) for key metrics
- Bars/charts: Grow from 0 to value (1.0s) with ease-out
- Pie charts: Clockwise fill (1.2s starting from 12 o'clock)

## Animation Timing Standards

### Slide Load Sequence

**Standard slide (data/text heavy):**
1. Background transition (0.6s)
2. Title fade in (0.4s)
3. Content sections stagger (0.15-0.3s between)
4. Visual elements last (0.6-0.8s)
5. **Total load time:** 2-3 seconds

**Hero slide (minimal content, big statement):**
1. Longer transition (1.0s)
2. Single element focus (1.5-2.0s)
3. Supporting text (if any) after long hold
4. **Total load time:** 3-5 seconds

---

### Presentation Pacing (Live Delivery)

**Slide dwell time:**
- Cover slide: 5-10 seconds (set tone, let audience settle)
- Problem/insight slides: 45-60 seconds (build tension, land insight)
- Data slides: 30-45 seconds (give time to read, process)
- Story slides: 60-90 seconds (human connection, emotional beats)
- Closing slide: 30 seconds minimum (hold for Q&A setup)

**Total deck:** 12-15 minutes (investor standard)  
**Rehearsed timing:** 11 minutes (allow buffer for interruptions)

## Technical Specifications

### Export Formats

**Keynote:**
- Magic Move transitions for smooth element animation
- Timing: Set all auto-advance off (presenter control)
- Build order: Clear sequence per slide

**PowerPoint:**
- Morph transition (Office 365) for smooth animation
- Custom animation pane: Define precise timing
- Presenter view: Notes with animation cues

**PDF (Static):**
- No animations (export final frame of each slide)
- Ensure all elements visible (no hidden builds)
- Print-quality resolution (300 DPI)

**Web/Video:**
- Export as video (1080p minimum, 4K preferred)
- 60fps for smooth animation
- Audio track optional (ambient/minimal)

---

## Animation Don'ts (Anti-Patterns)

**Never:**
- Slide in from sides (feels PowerPoint 2003)
- Bounce effects (childish, not luxury)
- Spinning text (disorienting, unprofessional)
- Excessive particle effects (distracting)
- Looping animations (nauseating in presentation)
- Sound effects (cheesy, breaks luxury tone)

**Exception:** Minimal ambient sound/music (if presenting live and venue appropriate)

## Brand Consistency in Motion

### Color Animation Rules

**Gold Accent (#D4AF37):**
- Use for emphasis animations (scale, glow, highlight)
- Never more than 1-2 Gold elements per slide
- Appears on key metrics, CTAs, "Synqra" mentions

**Teal Signature (#008B8B):**
- Use for secondary emphasis, progress indicators
- Technical elements, validation checkmarks
- Appears on data visualizations, charts

**Matte Black (#0A0A0A):**
- Background transitions (fade to black between sections)
- Grounding element (anchor all designs)

**Dust (#F5F5F0):**
- Text color (high contrast on black)
- Breathing room (light backgrounds for data tables)

---

### Typography Animation

**Headers (Inter Bold / Helvetica Neue Bold):**
- Fade in, never slide
- Scale effect max 1.05x (subtle, not dramatic)
- Hold minimum 1.0s before supporting text appears

**Body (Inter Regular):**
- Stagger in (0.15s between paragraphs/bullets)
- Never letter-by-letter (unless luxury moment on key quote)
- Line-by-line acceptable for lists

**Editorial (Times New Roman):**
- Reserved for pull quotes, founder story, luxury moments
- Slower reveal (0.3-0.5s per word)
- Hold longer (1.5-2.0s after full quote visible)
