## Why Competitors Can't Easily Follow

### 1. **Platform Lock-In Prevents Model Flexibility**
- Jasper/Copy.ai built on OpenAI partnerships
- Contractual obligations limit multi-model routing
- Business model tied to single-vendor pricing

### 2. **Technical Complexity**
- Building routing intelligence requires deep ML expertise
- Brand learning layer is non-trivial
- Multi-modal coordination (text + image + video) complex

### 3. **First-Mover Advantage in Orchestration**
- Synqra's routing algorithm trained on real usage
- Brand intelligence dataset grows with scale
- Network effects: more users = smarter system

### 4. **Luxury Positioning Hard to Copy**
- Competitors positioned as "cheap AI tools"
- Synqra positioned as "luxury orchestration"
- Brand perception moat
