## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks:
- **Model provider dependencies:** Diversify across 8+ providers, no single point of failure
- **API pricing changes:** Cost optimization layer insulates users from volatility
- **Scaling infrastructure:** Cloud-native architecture, designed for 100K+ users

### Market Risks:
- **Competition emerges:** First-mover advantage + data moat = 12-18 month lead
- **Market adoption slow:** Already validated with founder as Customer Zero
- **Enterprise sales cycle long:** Balance with creator/SMB growth for cash flow

### Execution Risks:
- **Founder burnout:** Hire senior operators to scale beyond founder-led motion
- **Team scaling:** Slow, intentional hiring (quality over speed)
- **Cash management:** Operate lean, profitable unit economics from Day 1
